Department of 'Data Science' launched at United University

own reporter Dhaka

Published: 17 May 2023

Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) of United International University (UIU) has been approved for the first ever graduate program in Data Science in Bangladesh. Classes will begin in the fall semester of 2023. This information was given in a press release.

According to the notification, the department has already set up high-capacity GPU-based computing facilities, which along with regular laboratories and other facilities are essential for the successful execution of the programme.
Faculty in this department have institutional degrees and experience in machine learning, deep learning, artificial intelligence and robotics.

UIU Vice-Chancellor (Acting) Prof. Abul Kashem Mia said, 'The challenge of the future is to make the graduates proficient in computing and data analysis. This expertise will support data-driven leadership in the National Roadmap for Smart Bangladesh. We believe that the graduates of this newly accredited program will play a key role in this process.'

Computer Science and Engineering Department Head Professor Salekul Islam said, 'Data science will be the most demanding subject in the near future. Numerous job opportunities will be created in the country and abroad. CSE department of UIU has taken timely decision. We are confident in producing the best graduates. They will lead our country in various fields of artificial intelligence and machine learning.'
